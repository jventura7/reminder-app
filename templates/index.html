Oops, Almost Forgot!

Welcome to Almost Forgot!, the web app that helps you stay connected and take care of your loved ones!

Concept

Creating an app that has permitted individuals, in this case guardians, to send reminders to a user, senior citizens, and prompt their phone would make this tedious process much simpler and faster. Our goal for this system is to make a program that makes sure to allow senior citizens to remember important tasks and allow guardians to still play a vital role by being able to create these reminders for them. This can help combat memory loss that is often associated with aging. and is significant because it's extremely common for the elderly to forget some of the daily tasks which can often lead to depression due to the lack of stimulus.
